Refugees Statistics

The number of refugees, particular in Europe, has grown considerably over the last decades as war, persecution and poverty continues to drive people from their homes - and the numbers are still rising. This website shows facts and figures about refugees in Europe.

Major Countries

This page shows the major countries regarding the total number of refugees for the year 2015. You can explore both data for the origin countries and for the destination countries as well. To compare the countries take a look at the bar chart or see the location of the countries on the map.

View visualization »

Timeline

This page shows the changing number of refugees during time from 2008 to 2015. You can look at the yearly view to see the upward trend and take a deeper look - the detailed view - to look into the monthly fluctuations. To compare the months of different years take a look at the monthly view. For all views you can choose your own interval to explore!

View visualization »

GDP

This page shows the influence of the destination country's GDP for its number of refugees. Use the list to select the countries you want to explore. Moreover you can select a year or even start a simulation to see the changes over time. To get a better view you can also hide the outlier countries. To get the concrete numbers for a specific country just hover the dot.

View visualization »

Distance

On this page we show if refugees prefer closer destination countries. You can filter and select the origin countries you want to explore. The visualisations show the correlation for the set of countries you have selected and you can even take a look at the correlation for a specific country by hovering it. To get the concrete numbers for a specific pair of countries you can click on the dot.
